## Research Focus

**Numerical Analysis**  
Developing efficient computational methods for solving differential equations, with emphasis on stability, accuracy, and practical application. Interested in multistep methods, temporal discretization schemes, and stiff system solvers.

**Online Learning**  
Algorithms that learn from streaming or sequential data, adapting in real-time. This includes bandit algorithms, regret minimization, and preference-based learning frameworks. Applications range from sports analytics to decision-making systems.

**Computer Vision**  
Visual recognition, image analysis, and pattern recognition using machine learning approaches. From classical computer vision to deep learning architectures for practical computer vision problems.

**Scientific Computing**  
Numerical solutions to real-world problems: environmental modeling (wildfire spread), physical simulations, geospatial analysis. Committed to high-performance methods and effective scientific visualization.
